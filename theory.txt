This is the basis for a theorem which relates to the numbers divisible by 3, 6, and 9 and those adjacent to them.
The definitions are as follows:
  TriNums == numbers divisible by 3, 6, or 9
    sub-class γ == numbers divisible by 3
    sub-class ζ == number divisible by 6
    sub-class ι == numbers divisible by 9
  α == numbers which come after a 3, 6, or 9
  β == numbers which come before a 3, 6, or 9

The following are a set of rules which are demonstrable at small numbers. The purpose of this pythons script is to test at scale:
## γ==3, ζ==6, ι==9, α==post, β==pre
any α + α = β
any α * α = α
any β + β = α
any β * β = α
any α * γ = γ
any β * γ = ζ
any α * ζ = ζ
any β * ζ = γ
